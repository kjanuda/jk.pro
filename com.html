Welcome to the Online Store for Cosmetics and Beauty Products

The Online Store for Cosmetics and Beauty Products is a web-based initiative that serves as an online marketplace for the sale of cosmetics and various beauty products. Customers within the cosmetics and beauty industry can explore, browse, and purchase products through this web application. It provides a user-friendly interface, built using the Bootstrap Framework and the AdminLTE Template, offering a range of features and functionalities, including online payments facilitated by the PayPal API.

This initiative encompasses two distinct user interfaces:

  • Administration Panel: Reserved for management personnel, the administration panel provides access to administrators. Administrators oversee critical data within the system, including order and product lists. They also have the capability to manage product inventories based on variants.
  • Client-Side: Accessible to all users, the client-side serves as the public face of the online store. However, some features are exclusive to registered users.

Technology Stack

The Online Store for Cosmetics and Beauty Products is powered by a robust technology stack:

  • Language Used: PHP, implemented with Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) principles.
  • Database Used: MySQL, providing a secure and efficient data storage solution.
  • Design Interface: Utilizing Bootstrap, JavaScript, HTML, Ajax, and JQuery for an attractive and interactive user experience.
  • Browser Compatibility: Compatible with popular browsers, including Opera, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, and IE8.
  • Software Environment: Deployable on WAMP, XAMPP, LAMP, and MAMP servers, ensuring flexibility and scalability.

The Online Store for Cosmetics and Beauty Products is a testament to the potential of modern web technologies in providing a seamless shopping experience for customers in the cosmetics and beauty industry.
