Welcome to the Online Motorcycle Parts & Services System

The Online Motorcycle Parts & Services system offers a user-friendly interface with two distinct sides. The first side is the Administration Panel, exclusively available to management personnel. Access to this side of the system requires valid staff or admin user credentials.

Within the Administration Panel, there are two user roles:

  • Staff User: Staff users have some restrictions on their access and actions within the system.
  • Admin User: Admin users have full access and control over all functions and data handled by this side of the system.

Management, represented by admin users, can efficiently manage various aspects of the system, including items, services, inquiries, orders, and more.

Technology Stack

The Online Motorcycle Parts & Services system is built upon a robust technology stack:

  • Language Used: PHP for dynamic web functionality.
  • Database Used: MySQL for secure and efficient data storage and retrieval.
  • Design Interface: Utilizing Bootstrap, JavaScript, HTML, Ajax, and JQuery for an appealing and interactive user experience.
  • Browser Compatibility: Compatible with popular browsers, including Opera, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, and IE8.
  • Software Environment: Deployable on WAMP, XAMPP, LAMP, and MAMP servers for flexibility and scalability.

The Online Motorcycle Parts & Services system empowers management with comprehensive control over their motorcycle parts and services operations, exemplifying the potential of modern web technologies in streamlining business processes.
